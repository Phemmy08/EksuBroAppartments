💡 Essential Guide for Students

Everything you need to know before renting 📚

🏠

Before Renting

  • ✓ Visit the apartment physically before making any payment
  • ✓ Check proximity to campus and major landmarks
  • ✓ Inspect water supply, electricity, and security
  • ✓ Verify the landlord's identity and ownership
  • ✓ Ask about additional costs (light, water, waste, security)
  • ✓ Take photos/videos of the apartment condition
🤝

Dealing with Agents

  • ✓ Always verify agent credentials before transacting
  • ✓ Request to see multiple options within your budget
  • ✓ Clarify agency fees upfront (typically 10-20% of annual rent)
  • ✓ Get all agreements in writing
  • ✓ Never pay full amount without signed agreement
  • ✓ Request receipt for every payment made
  • ✓ Keep communication records (chat history)
💰

Payment Tips

  • ✓ Budget includes: Rent + Agent Fee + Caution Fee + Utility Deposits
  • ✓ Negotiate payment plans if possible
  • ✓ Insist on meeting the actual landlord
  • ✓ Get official receipts with landlord's signature
  • ✓ Verify bank account details before transfers
  • ✓ Keep copies of all receipts and agreements
⚠️

Red Flags to Avoid

  • ✗ Agents demanding full payment before viewing
  • ✗ Deals that seem "too good to be true"
  • ✗ Refusal to provide written agreements
  • ✗ Pressure to make immediate decisions
  • ✗ Agents unwilling to show property ownership proof
  • ✗ No physical office or verifiable contact
  • ✗ Poor communication or evasive answers

🛡️ Safety & Security Tips

Stay safe while finding your perfect home 🏡

🔒

Security

Check for secure doors, windows, and compound fencing. Ask about neighborhood security.

🚨

Emergency

Know the location of nearest police station and hospital. Save emergency contacts.

👥

Neighbors

Meet potential neighbors and ask about the area. Join student housing groups.

📝

Documentation

Keep all documents safe. Take photos of property condition at move-in.
